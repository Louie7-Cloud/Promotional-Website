About the Project

The Municipality of Buug, located in the province of Zamboanga Sibugay, is a land of vibrant culture and immense natural beauty. This website serves as a digital window for locals and tourists alike to explore our history, meet our dedicated leaders, and discover the hidden gems of our town.
